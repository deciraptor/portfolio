About

Learn more about me.

My web development training was much more than a technical learning experience: it was the spark that allowed me to gain confidence in my abilities. By getting hands-on with code (Java, Angular, JavaScript), I discovered a real passion for building concrete solutions, combining pure logic with the creativity needed to design useful and ergonomic applications.

Beyond writing code, these projects introduced me to the realities of teamwork. I learned to master essential tools like Git for version control and Jira for agile tracking.

This solid technical foundation and ease in the digital environment naturally led me to more complex fields: networks and cybersecurity. Today, I leverage this experience to better understand the systems I aim to protect.
